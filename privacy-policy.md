# Privacy Policy
                                                                                                                                                                
**Last updated: March 19, 2026**                                                                                                                              
   
FamJam is operated by Patrick Hooper. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.                                                     
                                                            
## Information We Collect                                                                                                                                     
                                                            
  - **Name** — used to identify family members within the app                                                                                                   
  - **Email address** — used for account authentication     
  - **Phone number** — optional, used solely to send SMS notifications about household task activity                                                            
  - **Household activity data** — tasks, points, and rewards created and managed within your family                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                
## How We Use Your Information                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                
  - To operate the app and authenticate users               
  - To send SMS notifications to parents when children submit tasks or request rewards
  - We do not sell, share, or distribute your information to any third party                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                
## SMS Notifications                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                
Phone numbers are collected voluntarily during account signup. By providing a phone number you consent to receive SMS notifications related to household task 
activity in FamJam. You may opt out at any time by removing your phone number from your account.
                                                                                                                                                                
## Data Storage                                                                                                                                               
   
Your data is stored securely via Supabase. We do not store SMS message content.                                                                               
                                                            
## Children's Privacy                                                                                                                                         
                                                            
FamJam is a private household application. Child accounts are created and managed exclusively by a parent or guardian within the same household.
